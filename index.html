About Us

We are a community of people who are crazy about the world of Competitive Programming or CP for short and want to spread the word about CP in every college.


Every year we take part in ICPC, it is a world renowned coding competition where teams from all over the world come to compete.


You don't need to be well-versed in DSA and competitive programming at all to join our committee, as long as you show as that you have the drive to learn, you are welcome in the committee.

What Is ICPC?

The International Collegiate Programming Contest is an algorithmic programming contest for college students. Teams of three, representing their university, work to solve the most real-world problems, fostering collaboration, creativity, innovation, and the ability to perform under pressure. It is the oldest, largest, and most prestigious programming contest in the world.
